Privacy Policy for PyMate

Effective Date: December 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how LeMillion Labs collects, uses, and discloses your personal data when you use our Python learning mobile application, "PyMate".

1. Data We Collect

We collect the following personal and non-personal data necessary for the proper functioning and security of the App and to provide premium services.

  • Email Address: Collected during account registration and used for account identification, login, password recovery, and communication.
  • Account Status (Boolean Flag): We store a true/false status to determine if your account has paid for and unlocked the premium lessons. This is used solely for core app functionality.
  • Progress Data: Information regarding lessons completed, scores achieved, and last logged-in time. Used solely to maintain and synchronize your learning progress across devices.
  • Device Identifiers and Usage Logs: Information automatically collected by our third-party authentication and hosting services (e.g., IP address, device type, crash logs) for security and maintenance.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following primary purposes:

  • To **Provide Core Services** and App Functionality (logging in, tracking progress).
  • To **Process Premium Access** (checking the boolean flag after payment verification).
  • To **Maintain Account Security** and prevent unauthorized access.
  • To **Communicate** with you regarding your account or security updates.

3. Third-Party Services (Data Sharing)

We rely on the following third-party services which may process or store your data:

A. Firebase (Authentication, Hosting, and Database)

We use Google's **Firebase** platform to securely handle user authentication and host our database (including your email and progress data). Firebase collects data such as:

  • Email address and securely hashed passwords.
  • Device identifiers and usage metadata for crash reporting and security.

For more information, please see the Firebase Privacy and Security documentation.

B. Paystack (Payment Processing)

When you purchase premium access, the transaction is handled by **Paystack**. We **do not** collect or store your sensitive financial data (credit card numbers, bank details). That information is collected directly and securely by Paystack.

  • We share transaction details (e.g., your Email and the purchase amount) with Paystack to verify your payment and update your account status.

For more information, please see the Paystack Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention and Security

We retain your data as long as your account is active. We take reasonable measures, including relying on Firebase's security protocols (like encryption-in-transit via SSL/HTTPS), to protect your personal data from unauthorized access.
